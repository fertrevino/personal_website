This year I was fortunate to visit the nature near Berchtesgaden, a small town in the south of Germany. The beauty of its lakes and mountains is amazing.

I highly recommend hiking in the nearby national park and a boat tour in the Königssee. The scenery is particularly beautiful in summer. This is a photo I took during a hike in the park.

![A mountain in the Berchtesgaden National Park](/posts/berchtesgaden.jpeg)

I hope to return one day. Nature has a calming effect, and mountains are my favorite getaway.
