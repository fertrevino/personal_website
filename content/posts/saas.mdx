A few months ago, I waited 10 minutes for a physical menu at a Korean restaurant. This felt unproductive for both customers and businesses. Many local businesses either create their own static pages or lack digital menus altogether. They would benefit from an easy way to put their menus online.

So I built a web application for small restaurants to manage their food menus digitally. It lets managers and owners edit their menus and share them with customers via QR codes.

You can try it out at [menuop.com](https://menuop.com).

Looking forward to helping food business owners.
